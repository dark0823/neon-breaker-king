Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Neon Breaker King ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our game ("the Game") on the TikTok platform.

Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All gameplay data (scores, progress) is stored locally on your device or handled by the TikTok platform's built-in services (such as leaderboards).

Through the TikTok platform, certain information may be collected by TikTok as described in TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

We do not have access to or process any personal data. Game scores submitted to leaderboards are managed entirely by TikTok's platform services.

Third-Party Services

The Game is distributed exclusively through the TikTok platform and uses TikTok Mini Game SDK services for leaderboards, storage, and ads. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy for details on how TikTok handles your data.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data handling is managed by TikTok's platform in compliance with applicable laws.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the TikTok Developer Platform or via the contact information provided in the Game's store listing.
